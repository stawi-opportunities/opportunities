Questions, answered

What happens if I don't find a job?
Cancel any time. Starter and Pro are month-to-month. Managed is a rolling engagement — if you stop hunting, your agent steps back and you cancel when you're ready.
How is Pro different from Starter?
You get 5× the matches (25/week instead of 5), your profile is placed ahead of Starter subscribers in the matching queue, and every match comes with a draft cover letter. Both receive the same email-based support.
What exactly does the Managed agent do?
They review your CV, write cover letters, apply on your behalf, prep you for interviews, coach you through salary negotiation, and give you a direct line for same-day responses. Think of it as renting an experienced recruiter who works for you, not an employer.
Is the Free tier really free?
Yes. No credit card, no trial. You browse, save, and search forever without paying. You just don't get the matching engine or an agent.
Do you actually apply to jobs on my behalf?
Only on the Managed tier. Starter and Pro surface matches — every listing still links straight to the employer's application page so you control what gets sent. We never auto-submit without your say-so.
What payment methods work?
Credit and debit cards, plus mobile money in selected markets (KES, NGN, ZAR). Prices shown in USD.
