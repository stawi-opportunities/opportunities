Questions

Can I cancel any time?
Yes. Starter and Pro are month-to-month. Managed is a rolling engagement you pause or cancel when your search wraps up.
What counts as a "match"?
A role our matching engine (or your agent, on Managed) believes fits your CV, target title, seniority, region, and salary. You see them on your dashboard and in your weekly email digest.
How fast are matches delivered?
Starter matches are queued and delivered in a weekly digest. Pro subscribers jump the queue and get near-real-time notifications as roles come in. Managed subscribers get whatever cadence their agent agrees.
Does Pro get priority support?
No — Starter and Pro both receive email support. Only the Managed tier comes with a direct line and same-day responses.
What payment methods?
Credit and debit cards, plus mobile money in selected markets (KES, NGN, ZAR). Prices shown in USD.
Is the Free tier really free?
Yes — no credit card, no trial clock. Browse, search, and save jobs without paying a cent.
