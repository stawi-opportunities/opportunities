Questions

Can I cancel any time?
Yes. Starter and Pro are month-to-month. Managed is a rolling engagement you pause or cancel when your search wraps up.
What counts as a "match"?
A role our matching engine (or your recruiter, on Managed) believes fits your CV, target title, seniority, region, and salary. You see them on your dashboard and in your weekly digest.
How fast are matches delivered?
Starter is a weekly digest. Pro subscribers jump the queue and receive near-real-time notifications. Managed runs at whatever cadence your recruiter agrees with you.
Does Pro get priority support?
Starter and Pro both receive email support. Only Managed includes a direct line and same-day responses.
What payment methods work?
Credit and debit cards, plus mobile money in selected markets (KES, NGN, ZAR). Prices shown in USD.
Do you apply to jobs for me?
Only on Managed. Starter and Pro surface matches; every listing links to the employer's own application page so you control what gets sent.
