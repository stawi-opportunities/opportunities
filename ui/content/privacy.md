_Last updated: April 2026._

This policy explains what data Stawi Jobs ("we", "us") collects when you
use jobs.stawi.org, why we collect it, and what we do with it.

## Data we collect

- **Account data** — your email address, display name, and (if you create
  one) a profile photo, provided by your identity provider via OpenID
  Connect.
- **Profile data** — details you provide during onboarding: target job
  title, experience level, job-search status, salary range, preferred
  regions and time zones, country.
- **Usage data** — pages visited, jobs viewed, search queries, and saved
  jobs. Collected server-side from request logs.
- **Payment data** — for Premium subscribers only. Processed by our
  payment partner. We store a reference to your subscription, never card
  numbers or mobile-money PINs.

## How we use your data

- To match you with relevant job listings and show them on your
  dashboard.
- To send you optional weekly digest emails (you can opt out at any
  time).
- To improve our matching engine and product.
- To operate, secure, and debug the service.

## Sharing

We do not sell your data. We share limited data with:

- **Our identity provider** (Ory Hydra operated by Stawi) to authenticate
  you.
- **Our payment partner** to process Premium subscriptions.
- **Cloudflare**, our hosting and CDN provider.

We never share your profile or usage data with employers without your
explicit action (e.g. clicking "Apply" takes you to the employer's own
application page, outside our control).

## Retention

- Account and profile data: kept while your account is active and for 30
  days after deletion, then permanently purged.
- Usage logs: kept for 90 days, then aggregated and anonymised.
- Payment records: kept as required by applicable tax law.

## Your rights

You can, at any time:

- View and edit your profile from your dashboard.
- Delete your account and all associated data from your dashboard, or by
  emailing [hello@stawi.org](mailto:hello@stawi.org).
- Request an export of your data.
- Object to processing or withdraw consent (where processing is based on
  consent).

If you're in the EU/UK or a jurisdiction with similar laws (POPIA, CCPA),
these rights apply and you can exercise them by contacting us.

## International transfers

Our infrastructure is hosted on Cloudflare's global network. Your data
may be processed in any country where Cloudflare operates.

## Contact

Questions about this policy or your data:
[hello@stawi.org](mailto:hello@stawi.org).

## Changes

We'll update this page when the policy changes. Material changes will be
announced by email to active users before taking effect.
