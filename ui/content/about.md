## Our Mission

Stawi Jobs connects skilled professionals in Africa with top remote companies worldwide. We aggregate 37,000+ verified remote job listings, match them to your profile using AI, and apply on your behalf until you get hired.

## How We're Different

- **We apply for you** — Upload your CV, set your preferences, and we handle the applications. You focus on interviews.
- **Curated quality** — Every listing passes our quality gate. No spam, no expired posts.
- **AI-powered matching** — Our matching engine finds positions you're genuinely qualified for.
- **Africa-first** — Built for professionals in African time zones, with mobile money payment support.
