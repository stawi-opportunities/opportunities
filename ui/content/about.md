## Our Mission

Stawi Jobs connects skilled professionals with companies hiring worldwide. We aggregate verified job listings, match them to your profile with AI, and apply on your behalf until you get hired.

## How We're Different

- **We apply for you** — Upload your CV, set your preferences, and we handle the applications. You focus on interviews.
- **Curated quality** — Every listing passes our quality gate. No spam, no expired posts.
- **AI-powered matching** — Our matching engine finds positions you're genuinely qualified for.
- **Global by default** — Any role, any region, any work style. Your preferences, your filters.
