## Our mission

Stawi Jobs is a live, curated job board. We aggregate openings from companies
hiring worldwide, run them through a quality gate, and surface the ones most
relevant to you.

## How we're different

- **Curated quality** — every listing is checked for freshness, a legitimate
  employer, and complete details before it appears.
- **One profile, many matches** — set your target role, seniority, and regions
  once. We filter thousands of jobs down to the ones worth your time.
- **Weekly digests** — no noise. A single email with your best matches.
- **Global by default** — remote, hybrid, or on-site. Any region, any
  currency.

## What we don't do (yet)

We don't apply to jobs on your behalf. Every listing links straight to the
employer's own application flow so you control what goes in and when.
